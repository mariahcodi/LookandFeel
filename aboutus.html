About Us

We are Group 6 of Distributed Information System Course of Fall 2021 (ISM 6225) at the University of South Florida. We are pursuing an MS in Business Analytics & Data Science from the Muma College of Business. This assignment is intended to help us practice html, css, and JavaScript to get web development experience.

We learned some of the following while designing and developing this website:

  • Hands on experience with advanced HTML and CSS.
  • Implemented many new and latest CSS styling properties such as floating social icons, color changing menu, floating footer, pages consistency templatized, circular objects, tables, dropdowns, forms, Document Object Model (DOM) and many others
  • Mobile responsiveness, multi device adaptability, compressed images for uniform size and quickloading and maintaining consistency of look and feel.
  • Object Oriented Class Diagramming.

The project is available in the team Github Repository.
